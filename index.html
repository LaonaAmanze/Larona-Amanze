HI-TECH SUV

Range Rover Sport 2024

The 2024 Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV that combines performance, technology, and comfort.

Performance & Powertrains -Available engines include a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six (with mild-hybrid tech) and a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 in the high-performance SV edition. A plug-in hybrid (PHEV) version offers electric-only driving for short distances. Standard all-wheel drive (AWD) and adaptive air suspension ensure a smooth ride on and off-road.

Design & Interior -Sleek, modern exterior with flush door handles and slim LED headlights. High-quality materials inside, including leather, wood, and metal trims. 13.1-inch Pivi Pro infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto. Optional Meridian sound system, massage seats, and ambient lighting for a luxury feel.
Technology & Safety -Advanced driver assistance features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and 360-degree cameras. Off-road tech includes Terrain Response 2, which adjusts settings for different driving conditions. Optional rear-wheel steering for better maneuverability.
Price & Competition -Starts around P1,385,000 and goes up to P2,500,000+ for high-end models. Competes with the BMW X5, Mercedes GLE, and Porsche Cayenne.
